Instructions

  1. First, keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Next, move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop
  3. Now, move your right wrist in an ascending and decending manner. A red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. If so, press the play button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from laptop
  5. The red paddel is your paddel, which should move as per the movement of your right wrist
  6. Lastly, Have Fun!
